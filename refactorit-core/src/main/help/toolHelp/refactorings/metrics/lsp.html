Limited Size Principle (LSP)

This quality metric determines the number of direct subpackages of a package. Packages should have a limited amount of direct subpackages in order to avoid a project solution where too many elements are placed in one package. Packages with many elements are hard to maintain and will consequently lower the quality of the whole project.

RefactorIT recommends that packages with more than 10 subpackages are split up into smaller packages.
