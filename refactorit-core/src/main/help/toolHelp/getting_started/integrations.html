RefactorIT Integrations

RefactorIT can be integrated with the following IDE's:

  • Eclipse - version 3.0 or greater

  • JBuilder - Borland JBuilder 6 or greater

  • JDeveloper - Oracle JDeveloper 9 or greater

  • Netbeans - version 3.5-4.0 or greater

  • Stand Alone - stand alone version is also available
